Introduction to StayFit - Your Health Companion!

StayFit Health Companion now unifies physical activity data from Apple Health with your clinical records. A lightweight iOS collector transforms HealthKit metrics (steps, heart-rate, sleep, workouts) into FHIR R4 Observation resources and streams them—via secure HTTPS—into a dedicated "apple-health-records" index in OpenSearch.

Once ingested, the platform's existing analytics and AI engines treat these records like any other medical data, so users and clinicians see one continuous timeline that spans Apple Watch, iPhone, labs, medications, and diagnoses in the same dashboard.

Standards-based

HealthKit data is converted to FHIR using open-source tooling backed by Apple's Health Records API

Real-time ingestion

A serverless Lambda pipeline posts new Observations to OpenSearch in <5 seconds, enabling live trend charts

Single view

OpenSearch federates Apple Health and provider EHR indexes, letting AI Search answer questions that combine fitness and clinical context (e.g., "Did my resting heart-rate improve after my new hypertension medication?")

Security & compliance

Data stays encrypted end-to-end; no PHI touches Apple servers, and all Apple Health ingest events are logged for HIPAA audits

360° Health View: With Apple Health sync activated, StayFit Health Companion truly delivers a 360° view of both digital health records and everyday activity data—together, in one place.
