üåü Overview

StayFit Health Companion is a comprehensive digital health dashboard that provides AI-powered health insights, real-time data visualization, and comprehensive health analytics. Built with modern web technologies and deployed on AWS with enterprise-grade security and monitoring.

üçé Apple Health Integration

StayFit Health Companion now unifies physical activity data from Apple Health with your clinical records. A lightweight iOS collector transforms HealthKit metrics (steps, heart-rate, sleep, workouts) into FHIR R4 Observation resources and streams them‚Äîvia secure HTTPS‚Äîinto a dedicated "apple-health-records" index in OpenSearch.
