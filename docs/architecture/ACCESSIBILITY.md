## ‚ôø **Accessibility Compliance**

StayFit Health Companion is committed to providing an inclusive healthcare platform accessible to all users.

### **WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance**
- **Level AA conformance** with Web Content Accessibility Guidelines
- **Keyboard navigation** support throughout the application
- **Screen reader compatibility** with ARIA labels and descriptions
- **Color contrast ratios** meeting minimum 4.5:1 standards
- **Responsive design** supporting assistive technologies

### **Accessibility Features**
- **Alt text** for all images and icons
- **Focus indicators** for keyboard navigation
- **Skip navigation** links for screen readers
- **Semantic HTML** structure for better accessibility
- **High contrast mode** support
- **Text scaling** up to 200% without horizontal scrolling
